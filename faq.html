FAQ

Why not use fancy framework to make your website looks "better"?

I like the idea of KISS principle. I am in the field of medical imaging, we often talk about signal-to-noise ratio (SNR). The high SNR, the better. KISS principle is an idea to get high SNR, because the idea/content is the expected signal here instead of the design. Besides, I am not a professional front-end developer and don't want to waster too much time on the design. I will give you two articles influenced me on HTML design:Welcome to The HTML Hell Page and This is a Motherfucking Website

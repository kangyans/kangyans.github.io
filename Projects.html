Dynamic cardiac cine imaging

Cardiac magnetic resonance (CMR) plays an important role in clinically assessing cardiovascular diseases. However, CMR is inherently slow leading to patient discomfort and degraded image quality. In this project, we employ spiral k-space sampling strategies (especially Spiral bSSFP) to accelerate CMR acquisition and deep learning techniques for improved image reconstruction.

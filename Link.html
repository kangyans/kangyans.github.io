About Ph.D.

  • Terren Tao gives many great suggestions on this in his blog article Career Advice (academia). For general career advice, WAIT BUT WHY has a good article How to Pick a Career (That Actually Fits You)
  • Andre Karpathy: A Survival Guide to a Ph.D.
  • Philip J Guo: The Ph.D. Grind
  • John Baez: Advice for the Young Scientist
